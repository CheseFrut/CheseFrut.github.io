DESIRED QUALIFICATIONS

To be able to work with us you will be required to have some knowledge in web development. This knowledge can come from the following backgrounds:

  • GCSE in IT (or BTEC equivalent)
  • Level 2 award in Creating a Website
  • Level 2 Certificate in Interactive Media
  • Level 2/3 Diploma in Creative iMedia
  • Level 3 Diploma in Information Technology (Specialist)
  • Level 3 Diploma in IT User Skills

A valid qualification from our Endourced courses can also be accepted. Click HERE to visit our section about the courses we recommend.
